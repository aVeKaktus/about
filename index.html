About God's Mushroom

Founded in 2020, God's Mushroom emerged from a profound appreciation for the mystical and medicinal properties of fungi. Our mission is to connect people with the extraordinary world of mushrooms through carefully curated mystery boxes.

Our Vision

To create a global community of mushroom enthusiasts, fostering knowledge, appreciation, and responsible cultivation of fungi.

Our Values

Sustainability, education, and the sacred connection between humans and nature guide everything we do.

Our Promise

Every mystery box is thoughtfully curated to provide a unique and enriching experience in mycological discovery.
